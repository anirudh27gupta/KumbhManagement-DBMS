Ghat Locations

Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the oldest and holiest ghats, known for its spiritual energy and daily Ganga Aarti rituals.

Location: Dashashwamedh Ghat Rd, Varanasi

Bathing Timings: 4:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Safety Measures: Lifeguards, First Aid, Rescue Boats

Lifeguards Assigned: 10

Emergency Contact: +91 90123 45678

Kila Ghat

Located near Akbar’s Fort, this peaceful ghat offers a blend of historical charm and spiritual significance.

Location: Allahabad Fort, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 5:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Safety Measures: Lifeguards, Buoys, Warning Signs

Lifeguards Assigned: 7

Emergency Contact: +91 90234 56789

Rasulabad Ghat

Tranquil and ideal for sacred rites like Shraddha, this ghat offers a peaceful riverbank experience.

Location: Teliarganj, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 5:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Safety Measures: Rescue Boat, Medical Tent

Lifeguards Assigned: 6

Emergency Contact: +91 90345 67890

Naukayan Ghat

Known for serene boat rides and scenic beauty, this ghat offers a blend of devotion and leisure.

Location: Old Railway Bridge, Mutthi Ganj, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Safety Measures: Lifeguards, Life Jackets

Lifeguards Assigned: 5

Emergency Contact: +91 90456 78901

Mahewa Ghat

A serene and simple ghat perfect for quiet prayers and morning dips away from the crowds.

Location: Ram Nagar, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 4:30 AM - 7:30 PM

Safety Measures: Volunteers, First Aid Tent

Lifeguards Assigned: 4

Emergency Contact: +91 90567 89012

Saraswati Ghat

A vibrant ghat famous for its scenic beauty and cultural significance during the Kumbh Mela.

Location: Near New Yamuna Bridge, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 5:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Safety Measures: Lifeguards, Public Announcement System

Lifeguards Assigned: 8

Emergency Contact: +91 90678 90123

Gyan Ganga Ghat

Associated with wisdom and meditation, this ghat provides a peaceful space for spiritual seekers.

Location: Dewrakh Kachhar, Prayagraj

Bathing Timings: 4:00 AM - 8:30 PM

Safety Measures: Lifeguards, Emergency Response Tent

Lifeguards Assigned: 6

Emergency Contact: +91 90789 01234
